## üîç Conversion Funnel Analysis

### Funnel Health Check

```
Stage 1: New Leads ‚Üí Contacted
Target Contact Rate: >80%
If below: Staff not calling leads quickly

Stage 2: Contacted ‚Üí Hot Leads
Target Hot Lead Rate: >40%
If below: Improve call quality/pitch

Stage 3: Hot Leads ‚Üí Joined
Target Conversion: >60% of hot leads
If below: Review joining process

Overall Conversion: >25% of all leads
```

### Quick Fixes

**Low Contact Rate (<70%)**
- Redistribute leads
- Set daily call targets
- Monitor response times

**Low Hot Lead Rate (<30%)**
- Improve call scripts
- Train staff on qualification
- Review lead quality

**Low Conversion (<20%)**
- Simplify joining process
- Follow up faster
- Better closing techniques
