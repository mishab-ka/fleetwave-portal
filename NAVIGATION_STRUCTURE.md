## Navigation Paths

### Accommodation Section

- **Main Section**: `Accommodation` (with Home icon)
- **Room & Bed Management**: `/admin/room-bed-management`

  - Visual room/bed layout
  - Driver assignment/unassignment
  - Real-time occupancy status
  - Rent calculation display

- **Monthly Rent Dashboard**: `/admin/monthly-rent-dashboard`
  - Monthly rent summaries
  - Driver-specific rent tracking
  - Export functionality (CSV)
  - Occupancy rate calculations

## How to Access

1. **Login as Admin**: Navigate to the admin dashboard
2. **Find Accommodation**: Look for the "Accommodation" section in the sidebar
3. **Expand Section**: Click on "Accommodation" to see the sub-items
4. **Navigate**: Click on either:
   - "Room & Bed Management" for managing assignments
   - "Monthly Rent Dashboard" for reporting and analytics
